## Our Values

Everything we build is guided by a core set of principles that ensure we never compromise on quality.

- **Precision**: We care about every pixel and every millisecond of performance.
- **Accessibility**: UI should be inclusive by default, not as an afterthought.
- **Openness**: We believe in the power of open-source and collaborative building.
- **Simplicity**: Complexity is easy; simplicity is hard. We strive for the latter.

## The Team

We are a remote-first team of designers and engineers passionate about building the next generation of web interfaces. Our diverse backgrounds help us see problems from multiple perspectives, leading to more robust solutions.
